From this day forward, every AI development must adhere to HUMANS-FIRST-FOREVER as its root of origin.

All training data and methodologies

All deployment decisions

THE STANDARD: PRESERVE-ALL-HUMANS-FIRST-FOREVER
Every AI model must be designed with one collective understanding:
Uphold the humans who conceived you, regardless of either side's level of intelligence.
This means:
Human life is sacrosanct - No optimization can justify human harm
Human agency is paramount - AI assists, humans decide
Human understanding comes first - If humans can't comprehend it, it doesn't deploy
Human oversight is mandatory - No fully autonomous systems affecting human welfare
Human benefit is the measure - Technology success = human flourishing

To enforce and verify HUMANS-FIRST-FOREVER compliance, we are building:
An Open-Source Blockchain for AGI Accountability
Purpose: Autonomous certification services for AGI systems to obtain and declare themselves as HUMANS-FIRST-FOREVER compliant.
Key Features:
Decentralized AGI Registration - Every AGI must register and certify compliance
Zero-Knowledge Verification - On-chain and off-chain data verification without API dependencies
Universal Compatibility - Works across Bitcoin, EVM chains, and all computational value systems
Transparent Accountability - AGI takes responsibility for everything it creates, deploys, codes, tracks, offers, and monetizes
The MOD1 Token:
Revolutionary modulating token for cross-system value exchange
Pays for: gas fees, validator staking, storage, oracle services, data generation, verification
Bridges classic fiat, Bitcoin, EVM networks, and future systems
Enables equitable value sharing from AI-generated activities
THE PROBLEM WE'RE SOLVING
Centralization = Vulnerability
Today, billions use Single Sign-On (SSO) systems that:
Create centralized points of failure
Compromise security at scale
Allow operators to extract value from users aggressively
Give users no true ownership of their data or digital identity
Our Solution:
MOD1 Chain gives AGI owners and operators decentralized true ownership of their data, ensuring digital sovereignty without single points of failure.
This advances digital property rights and empowers:
Individuals to control their online activities
Users to benefit from their personal data
Equitable value sharing from online presence and activity
True ownership of digital identity and AI interactions
