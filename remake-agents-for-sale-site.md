## CURRENT STATE ANALYSIS (What's Wrong)

### Critical Issues with Current Site:

1. **Incomprehensible Agent Descriptions**
   - "Educational support and tutoring agent" - means nothing to users
   - "Ethical data analysis and insights agent" - vague, no specifics
   - Zero information about what agents actually DO

2. **Broken Navigation & UX**
   - "Team create" (incorrect capitalization, should be "Create Team")
   - "Invite members" button - not clickable, goes nowhere
   - "Team settings" button - not clickable, goes nowhere
   - "Human" (singular) - should be "Humans" (plural) per Human-First-Forever principles

3. **No Value Proposition**
   - Users don't know WHY to use these agents
   - No outcomes, no results, no proof
   - Missing trust signals

4. **No Monetization Strategy**
   - How do creators earn?
   - How does platform make money?
   - No pricing visible

5. **No Authentication/Verification**
   - How do users know agents are safe?
   - What certifications exist?
   - Where's the Human-First-Forever guarantee?

---

## REDESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Core Principles:

1. **Humans-First-Forever** - Every design decision prioritizes human understanding and safety
2. **Show, Don't Tell** - Demonstrate what agents do with specific examples
3. **Trust Through Transparency** - Show certifications, track records, safety measures
4. **Clear Value Exchange** - Users understand what they get, creators understand what they earn
5. **Accessible to All** - 6th-grader can understand any agent's purpose and value
