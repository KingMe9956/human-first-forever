## 1. Medical Diagnosis AGI Authenticator

### Must-Have Features (Extended to 20)

**Original Core Features (1-5):**
1. **Clinical Credential Verification** - Real-time validation of medical training data sources, ensuring AGI decisions trace back to peer-reviewed literature and approved clinical guidelines.
2. **Diagnostic Audit Trail** - Immutable logging of every diagnostic pathway, showing reasoning steps from symptoms to conclusions with timestamp and version control.
3. **Patient Safety Lockout** - Automatic suspension protocols when AGI outputs deviate beyond statistically acceptable ranges from human specialist consensus.
4. **HIPAA-Compliant Access Control** - Multi-factor biometric authentication with role-based permissions for healthcare providers accessing AGI insights.
5. **Hallucination Detection Engine** - Cross-references AGI recommendations against current medical databases to flag potentially fabricated drug names, dosages, or procedures.

**Extended Features (6-20):**
6. **Differential Diagnosis Ranking** - Presents multiple possible diagnoses with probability scores, forcing human physician final decision rather than single AGI conclusion.
7. **Contraindication Alert System** - Checks patient allergies, current medications, and conditions against proposed treatments in real-time.
8. **Second Opinion Requirement** - Mandates human specialist review for diagnoses with confidence below 85% or for life-threatening conditions.
9. **Pediatric Safety Scaling** - Automatically adjusts drug dosages and treatment protocols based on patient age, weight, and developmental stage.
10. **Geriatric Complication Prediction** - Flags treatments that may cause adverse effects in elderly patients based on age-related physiological changes.
11. **Cultural Competency Module** - Considers patient cultural background, language barriers, and health literacy in communication recommendations.
12. **Emergency Override Protocol** - Allows human physicians to immediately override any AGI recommendation with documented justification.
13. **Medical Imaging Verification** - Requires radiologist confirmation of AGI-detected abnormalities before diagnosis finalization.
14. **Drug Interaction Database** - Continuously updated connection to FDA and international pharmaceutical databases for latest safety information.
15. **Genetic Variant Interpretation** - Cross-references patient genomic data with ClinVar and other validated databases for accurate variant classification.
16. **Clinical Trial Matching** - Identifies relevant clinical trials for patients when standard treatments are ineffective or unavailable.
17. **Prior Authorization Assistance** - Generates evidence-based documentation for insurance approval of recommended treatments.
18. **Patient Communication Templates** - Provides physician-reviewed explanations of diagnoses and treatments at appropriate health literacy levels.
19. **Diagnostic Uncertainty Quantification** - Explicitly states confidence intervals and areas where evidence is limited or contradictory.
20. **Continuing Medical Education Integration** - Updates physicians on new research relevant to their AGI-assisted diagnoses for ongoing learning.
